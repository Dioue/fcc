Kirigaya Kazuto

The Black Swordsman


Achievements in Life

  • Sword Art Online -The only player with the ability to wield two swords at a time (called as "Dual Blade". With the skill, Kirito fought the leader of KoB (also known as Knights of the Blood) in the deal of freeing the thousands of players unable to log out.
  • Alfheim Online - After sword art online incident, kirito became the saviour of hundreds of players trapped in the new game Alfheim Online.
  • GunGale Online - Dress as a black, long-haired character. Kirito began the investigation of the simultaneous death on online mmorpg GGO (also known as Gun Gale Online).
  • Excalibur - Completed the quest "The Holy Sword of the Ice Palace". Kirito obtained the Holy Sword Excalibur that said to be the strongest sword in the game Alfheim Online.
